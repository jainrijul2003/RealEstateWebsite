FAQ

How to cancel a booking?

Log in to your account, go to the dashboard, and find the booking you want to cancel. Click on the cancel button, and follow the instructions to complete the cancellation process.

When will I get possession of the property?

The possession date depends on the specific property and the terms of the sale. You can find information about possession in the property listing or contact the seller for details.

Where can I pay the rent for a property?

Rent payments can be made through our online portal. Log in to your account, go to the 'My Rentals' section, and follow the instructions to make a secure online payment.

How to contact potential buyers for my property?

Once your property is listed, interested buyers can contact you through our platform. Make sure your contact information is up-to-date in your account settings for smooth communication.

Why is my listing not showing up in search results?

If your listing is not showing up, check if it meets our guidelines. Ensure that all necessary details are filled out, and the images are clear. Contact our support team if the issue persists.

How can I promote my property listing for better visibility?

To enhance the visibility of your listing, consider upgrading to premium features, adding high-quality images, and providing detailed property descriptions.
